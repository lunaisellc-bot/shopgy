Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to upload products manually?

No. We connect via Etsy API. All photos, descriptions, and prices are fetched automatically.

Does Etsy approval take time?

Yes, for security, Etsy reviews requests. This takes about **2-3 days**.

During this time, if they ask "Is this for commercial use?" or "Did you write the code?", don't worry! We provide you with the exact "Copy-Paste" answers that guarantee approval.

How does payment work?

Customers click "Buy" on your site and are redirected to Etsy checkout. You don't need a separate payment gateway.

Does this help my ranking?

Yes! Bringing external traffic from your own domain is a huge signal for Etsy's algorithm.
