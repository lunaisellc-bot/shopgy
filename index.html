Technical FAQ

How often does it sync with Etsy?

Depends on the plan. Business plan checks daily. Elite plan uses 'Webhooks' which triggers an update the second you change something on Etsy.

Do my Etsy reviews transfer?

We can embed your Etsy star rating badge to build trust, but we don't copy individual user review texts due to privacy policies.

What if I delete a product on Etsy?

The system will automatically archive it on your website during the next sync cycle, so you don't sell an item you don't have.
