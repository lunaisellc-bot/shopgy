Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to upload products manually?

No. We connect via Etsy API. All photos, descriptions, and prices are fetched automatically.

Does Etsy approval take time?

Yes. For security, Etsy reviews requests. This takes about 2–3 days.

During this time, if they ask "Is this for commercial use?" or "Did you write the code?", don’t worry. We provide ready-to-use guidance and suggested answers aligned with Etsy’s questions to help you respond confidently.

How does payment work?

Customers click “Buy on Etsy” and are redirected to Etsy checkout. You don’t need a separate payment gateway.

Does this help my ranking?

External traffic can support long-term visibility signals. Results vary based on product, niche, and overall shop quality.

What does “Lifetime Tech Warranty” include?

Critical bug fixes, uptime-related support, and security updates for the existing site structure. New features, extra pages, or major redesigns are handled as a separate request.
