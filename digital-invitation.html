Why Go Digital?

More Than Just An Invite

Digital invitations are not just eco-friendly; they are smarter. They solve the logistical headaches of organizing an event, leaving you to enjoy the excitement.

Zero Friction RSVP

Guests hate mailing back RSVP cards. With our system, they simply click "Attend", select their meal preference, and mention allergies in seconds.

*You get a neat list of everyone attending.

No One Gets Lost

"Where is the venue?" "Where do I park?" Stop answering these calls. We integrate Google/Apple Maps directly. One click navigates them to your door.

*Works perfectly on all mobile phones.

The "Gift" Question

Common Questions

Is it hard to share?

Not at all. We give you a unique link (e.g., sarahandtom.com). You just send it via WhatsApp, SMS, or Email. That's it.

Can older guests use it?

Yes. We design for simplicity. Big buttons, clear text. If they can use WhatsApp, they can use your invitation.

What about privacy?

Your event is private. We can add a password so only your guests can access the details.

Turnaround Time?

Standard designs are ready in 48 hours. Custom designs take 3-5 days. We move fast.
