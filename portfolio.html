How to choose

A good theme matches the buying decision.

A jewelry buyer needs detail and trust. A fashion buyer needs fit, lifestyle, and identity. A home decor buyer needs mood, scale, material, and room context. We start with buyer psychology, then shape the website.

1. Product type

The product determines what must be explained visually and structurally.

2. Traffic source

Pinterest, Instagram, Google, and ads need different landing priorities.

3. Etsy purchase flow

Every theme can send buyers to official Etsy listings for checkout.
