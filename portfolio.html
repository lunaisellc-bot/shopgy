How to choose

A good demo direction matches the buyer’s hesitation.

A jewelry buyer asks, “Can I trust the detail and gift meaning?” A fashion buyer asks, “Can I imagine myself wearing this?” A home decor buyer asks, “Will this feel right in my room?” VitrinY starts there.

1. Product type

The product determines what must be explained visually and structurally.

2. Traffic source

Pinterest, Instagram, Google, and ads need different landing priorities.

3. Etsy purchase flow

Every demo sends buyers to official Etsy listings for checkout.
