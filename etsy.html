Does VitrinY replace Etsy?

No. It strengthens your brand presence outside Etsy while purchase buttons direct buyers to your official Etsy listings.

Is this Shopify?

No. Shopify is for full independent ecommerce. VitrinY is lighter and built for sellers who still rely on Etsy checkout.

Can this help Instagram or Pinterest traffic?

Yes. A branded landing page gives external traffic a more focused and premium first stop before Etsy purchase intent.
