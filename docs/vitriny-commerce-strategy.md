# VitrinY Commerce Strategy

## Core Positioning

VitrinY is not a cheaper Shopify and it is not another Etsy add-on.

VitrinY is a brand layer for Etsy sellers: a professional website, product presentation, visual system, and traffic landing page — with purchases completed safely on Etsy.

## Primary Promise

Turn your Etsy shop into a brand without moving checkout away from Etsy.

## Target Customer

VitrinY is built for Etsy sellers who:

- Already have real products and active selling intent.
- Want a professional .com brand presence.
- Use Instagram, Pinterest, ads, or SEO traffic.
- Want stronger product presentation without leaving Etsy checkout trust.
- Are not ready to run a full independent Shopify operation.

## Not the Target Customer

VitrinY is not ideal for:

- Sellers with no products or no sales intent.
- Sellers only looking for the cheapest website builder.
- Brands that already need a full independent checkout, warehouse, and ecommerce stack.
- Sellers who want to move Etsy-originated buyers to off-platform payment.

## Product Architecture

### 1. VitrinY Commerce

The main product. A professional brand website and catalog system for Etsy sellers.

Key components:

- Brand homepage
- Product/category presentation
- Etsy listing links
- SEO-ready landing structure
- Trust messaging
- Analytics and pixel readiness
- Optional Etsy API catalog refresh

### 2. VitrinY Studio

Fast-cash product line. Visual and video systems for Etsy sellers.

Key components:

- Etsy listing image packs
- Product lifestyle mockups
- Pinterest pins
- Instagram/Reels assets
- Launch visual kits

### 3. VitrinY Invite

Secondary product line. Digital invitation and RSVP websites.

This should not dominate the homepage. It can remain as a separate landing page or future vertical.

## Safe Etsy Positioning

Do not position VitrinY as a way to avoid Etsy fees or move Etsy-originated transactions off-platform.

Correct positioning:

- External traffic lands on the seller's brand website.
- The website works as a premium catalog and brand layer.
- Purchase buttons send buyers to official Etsy listings.
- Checkout remains on Etsy.
