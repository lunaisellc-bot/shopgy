Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

1. Data Collection

At VitrinY, we respect your privacy. We collect only the data necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and phone number (if provided).
  • Etsy Shop Data: Through the official Etsy Open API, we access public listing data (titles, prices, images, descriptions) to construct your website. We do NOT access your payment credentials or private messages.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to generate and host your standalone website. We do not sell your customer lists or shop data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your website is hosted on secure global CDNs (e.g., Netlify/Vercel). We use industry-standard encryption (SSL) to protect data in transit.

Terms of Service

1. Services

VitrinY provides a service that converts Etsy shop listings into a standalone website. This is a one-time setup service. Hosting is provided via third-party providers.

2. Payments & Refunds

Since our products are digital setup services involving manual configuration and server deployment, all sales are final. We do not offer refunds once the domain deployment process has begun.

3. User Responsibilities

You are responsible for maintaining your Etsy shop. Since our system syncs with Etsy, if your Etsy shop is suspended, your VitrinY website may stop updating new products (though existing products will remain visible).
