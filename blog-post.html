The "Rental" Trap

When you sell exclusively on Etsy, you are technically "renting" space on their platform. While Etsy brings traffic, they also control the rules. They can increase fees, change algorithms, or even suspend shops without warning. This is the biggest risk for a growing business.

The Power of .COM

Having your own domain (e.g., yourbrand.com) signals to customers that you are a serious business, not just a hobbyist. It allows you to:

  • Run Ads Safely: Connect Facebook Pixel and TikTok Ads directly to your site to collect data.
  • Build Assets: Collect customer emails (your biggest asset) which Etsy doesn't allow easily.
  • Branding: Create a brand identity that sticks in memory, separating you from the millions of other sellers.
