How to Set Up a US LLC from Turkey (Without Visiting)

Stop letting payment gateways block your growth.

If you are an entrepreneur in Turkey, you know the pain: PayPal is banned, and Stripe is unavailable. This is the single biggest barrier to scaling a global Etsy or Shopify brand. But there is a legal "cheat code": The US LLC.

Why You Need an LLC

It's not just about looking professional. An LLC (Limited Liability Company) allows you to obtain an EIN (Employer Identification Number). With an EIN, you are no longer just a person in Turkey; you are a US entity.

The "Holy Trinity" Stack

To run a global business from Istanbul/Ankara, you need three things connected: Wyoming LLC + Mercury/Wise Bank + Stripe.

Step 1: Choose the Right State

Forget Delaware unless you plan to seek venture capital. For e-commerce solopreneurs, Wyoming or New Mexico are superior. They have low annual fees and strong privacy laws.

Step 2: Get Your EIN

Once your LLC is formed (takes 2-5 days), you must apply for an EIN from the IRS. This is the "Social Security Number" for your business. Note: As a non-US resident, this can take 15-30 days via fax processing. Patience is key.

Step 3: Banking & Payments

  • Wise Business: Use your LLC docs to open a Wise USD account. This gives you a US routing number to receive Etsy payments.
  • Stripe: Use your EIN and Wise account to activate Stripe. Now you can accept credit cards globally on your own website (Shopgy/Shopify).
